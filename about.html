ABOUT HOUSETHAT

HouseThat Interiors was founded in 2022 and has already reached a milestone as it possess high profile residential and commercial projects in and around Indian states like Maharashtra, Gujarat, delhi and Karnataka.

A Design firm focusing on high quality projects with a unique aesthetic. We take pride in selecting a project with high quality of attention to design detail and lets us explore and experiment the world class designs.

MEET THE FOUNDER

Anita Shah is the Founder & Principal Architect of HouseThat, a multidisciplinary architecture and design studio creating thoughtfully crafted residential, commercial, hospitality, and bespoke spaces across India and the UAE.

Raised in Nashik, Anita developed an early understanding of construction through her family's real estate and building business. She later pursued Architecture in Mumbai before completing a design fellowship in Italy, where she gained exposure to European design philosophy, craftsmanship, and luxury spatial planning.

She began her professional career in Mumbai before returning to Nashik to establish HouseThat. Today, her work is guided by one philosophy—to create spaces that are timeless, deeply personal, and built with purpose.
