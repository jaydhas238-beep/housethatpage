About HouseThat

A studio built on detail, restraint and intention.

HouseThat Interiors was founded in August 2022 and has worked on premium residential and commercial projects across Maharashtra, Gujarat, Delhi and Karnataka.

Meet The Founder

Anita Shah

Founder & Principal Architect

Anita Shah is the Founder & Principal Architect of HouseThat, a multidisciplinary architecture and design studio creating thoughtfully crafted residential, commercial, hospitality, and bespoke spaces across India and the UAE.

Raised in Nashik, Anita developed an early understanding of construction through her family’s real estate and building business, where she was exposed to the world of sites, materials, and execution from a young age. She later pursued Architecture in Mumbai before completing a design fellowship in Italy, where she gained exposure to European design philosophy, craftsmanship, and luxury spatial planning.

She began her professional career in Mumbai, working on luxury residential projects and contributing to the design and planning of homes for some of India’s most distinguished private clients and business families. This experience laid the foundation for her approach to refined design, meticulous detailing, and seamless execution.

Driven by a vision to create a more personal and holistic design practice, Anita returned to Nashik and founded HouseThat. What began as a boutique luxury interior studio has grown into a multidisciplinary practice offering architecture, interiors, turnkey execution, and design consultancy for residential, commercial, and hospitality projects across multiple cities in India and the UAE.

Today, her work is guided by a simple philosophy: to create spaces that are timeless, deeply personal, and built with purpose.
