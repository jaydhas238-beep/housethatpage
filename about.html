About HouseThat

A studio built on detail, restraint and intention.

HouseThat Interiors was founded in August 2022 and has worked on premium residential and commercial projects across Maharashtra, Gujarat, Delhi and Karnataka.

Founder & Principal Designer

Anita Shah

Anita Shah is the creative force behind HouseThat, bringing over five years of expertise in interior design and space planning. Her work blends timeless aesthetics with thoughtful functionality.

A graduate from SNDT University, Mumbai, with First Class First distinction and multiple project accolades, Anita founded HouseThat with a vision to create refined, personal and enduring spaces.

Philosophy

“We do not design for trends.
We design for the way people live.”
